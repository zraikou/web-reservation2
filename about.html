Our History

Since its grand opening in 2005, White Lotus has redefined luxury hospitality on Paradise Island. Designed by renowned architects Rico, Ivan and Erik, the resort was conceived as a harmonious blend of traditional elegance and contemporary innovation.

The name "White Lotus" was inspired by the pristine white lotus flowers that once grew abundantly in the lagoon that our property now overlooks. These flowers symbolize purity, rebirth, and enlightenment in many culturesâ€”values that we embrace in our approach to hospitality.

What began as a boutique resort with just 25 rooms has evolved into a world-class destination featuring 150 luxurious rooms and villas, award-winning restaurants, a state-of-the-art spa, and unparalleled service that keeps our guests returning year after year.

Our Mission & Values

At White Lotus, our mission is to create extraordinary experiences that delight our guests and exceed their expectations. We are guided by our core values that shape every interaction and decision.

Excellence

Sustainability
